The Virtual Boy controller is the controller that was released
specifically for the Virtual Boy. The controller, which is in the
shape of an M, features dual d-pads, six face buttons, and two left and
right trigger or shoulder buttons.

Nintendo 3D Virtual Boy HARD CASE

$299.99

This hard carrycase features a hard, shock resistant shell and sturdy
foam interior which is custom cut to hold the VB unit, stand, AC Adapter or Battery Pak,
controller and games. As this is specifically built for holding the VB, everything fits snug and perfect
and stays in place when the case is shut.The case was made by Southern Case,
of Raleigh, North Carolina. Besides producing the Virtual Boy carrying cases for
Nintendo, the now defunct company has been a supplier of hard shell plastic cases
to several camcorder manufacturers including Sharp, Canon, Panasonic and JVC.

Stereo Headphone

$149.99

The Stereo Headphone for Nintendo Virtual Boy is an essential accessory for any
Virtual Boy owner. This device plugs into the Virtual Boy’s headphone jack to provide users with a full sound
experience. The Virtual Boy’s sound capabilities are limited without the headphone, so this device is a must-have
for anyone wanting to fully enjoy the console’s games. The stereo sound enhances the gaming experience
by providing clear and crisp audio, allowing users to truly immerse themselves in the game. The Stereo Headphone
is lightweight and features an adjustable headband, making it
comfortable to wear for extended periods of gaming.
