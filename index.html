Qualities of Nintendo virtual boy

    Innovative: Nintendo Virtual Boy was the first console to feature 3D stereoscopic graphics and a unique design.
    Portability: The Virtual Boy was the first console to feature a fully portable design, making it easy to take with you wherever you go.
    Immersive: The Virtual Boy's red-and-black visuals and unique controls create an immersive experience that is hard to replicate.
    Affordable: The Virtual Boy was one of the most affordable consoles of its time, making it accessible to everyone.
    Games: The Virtual Boy featured a variety of high-quality games that are still enjoyed today
